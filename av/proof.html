This is what a publication feels like.

Not a blog with a header image. A room you walk into.

Behind these words, a quiet weather is happening. Heat blobs orbit slow Lissajous paths. Every ~38 seconds one of them pulses — pressure releasing without warning, without strobing, without asking for your attention. The motion is felt, not noticed.

Audio is off by default. You enabled nothing. You opted into nothing. Try the toggle in the bottom right when you're ready — a single low drone that won't interrupt your reading. Or don't. The atmosphere works without it.

If your operating system requests reduced motion, the engine respected that at boot. A single static frame is rendered. You can opt back in.
