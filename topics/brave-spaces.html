Topic

Brave Spaces

Rooms built to hold discomfort without tipping into harm — where people can be honest enough that something actually changes.

Failure-naming becomes structurally ordinary when the container makes individual courage unnecessary. 2026-05-31The Load-Bearing WallSafe spaces protect people from discomfort.
