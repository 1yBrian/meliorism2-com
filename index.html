What Meliorism2.com is

A practitioner media publication for trainers, coaches, and educators. Each issue names one specific obstacle — not a concept, not a theory — the actual mechanism getting in your clients' way right now. Then gives you language to reach for in the room.

The operating premise

Meliorism 2.0 holds that the better version of a person, a group, or a practice is already present — concealed by friction, circumstance, and habit. Each issue names one specific thing getting in your clients' way. Traces its mechanism. Gives you language to reach for in the room — today, if you need it.

What this is

Four things that set the standard

01 — Format

Format Explorers

Each issue arrives in a distinct container — Field Notebook, Ship's Log, Intelligence Dossier. The format is the argument. The structure is the signal. Not a newsletter. A practicum.

02 — Voice

Practitioner-to-Practitioner

Written by someone who works in the room, for people who work in the room. No academic distance. No executive summary veneer. Peer-level honesty about what actually happens when the work gets hard.

03 — Standard

Research-Grounded

Every issue cites its sources. The mechanism is traced from primary research to practitioner application. You can follow the thread, check the evidence, and adapt the insight with confidence.

04 — Design

Neurodivergent-Friendly

High contrast. Legible type. Short issues with one thesis, one mechanism, one takeaway. Designed for practitioners whose own cognition is part of the work — not an obstacle to it.
