## Why This Exists

The Design Engine's promise is 365 issues that never feel alike. That promise is only keepable if the system *remembers* what it has already done. This ledger is that memory.

**Enforcement rules (checked by the engine before finalizing a spec):**
- No identical `(container × primitive)` pair ever repeats.
- No same `primary hue` within a rolling 7-issue window.
- No same `container family` within a rolling 14-issue window.
- No same `archetype` as the immediately prior issue.

On collision, the engine regenerates the colliding layer and re-checks.

**Quarterly use:** this table is the spine of the investor/template report — proof, row by row, that every issue was a distinct designed object.
